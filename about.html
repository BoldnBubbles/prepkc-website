About

Our mission is to create and implement strategies that help students attending Kansas City's urban school districts succeed in college and their careers.

PREP-KC partners with 6 districts and 4 charters; enrolling over 68,000 students; 81% students of color and 84% low-income, all representing culturally rich, diverse, talented, communities across the heart of Kansas City.

  • 3,800 annual high school graduates
  • 20 high schools and 15 middle schools
  • 19 of the 20 zip codes with the lowest median household incomes
