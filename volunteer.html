VIRTUAL

PREP-KC supports online career exploration opportunities using our Connector platform. Each session is created by teachers to have volunteers speak about a variety of requested topics. Note that the platform will require creating a free account and a free virtual background check to be completed (conducted by a third party with no cost to the volunteer).
