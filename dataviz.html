DataViz KC

Kansas City's premier high school data visualization competition, challenging students to tell compelling stories with data while developing critical analytical and presentation skills.

Unlock the Power of Data Visualization

Join our DataViz Competition, designed for high school students and teachers to explore data visualization. Transform complex data into engaging visual stories, with categories for every passion and a focus on community impact within the Greater Kansas City Region. Dive into the world of data and make your insights count!

The DataViz competition offers two divisions: "Follow Your Passion" for high school students to explore topics of their choice and "Community Impact" for both middle and high school students focusing on the Greater Kansas City Region. Eligibility extends to teams of up to 5, including teachers and students from PREP-KC partner districts. The competition window spans from September 28th to March 31st, with a $2,500 prize pool.

Learn More & Register Explore Data Science Initiative

DataViz KC is part of PREP-KC's comprehensive Data Science Initiative, helping students develop data literacy skills. Winners can earn Market Value Assets toward college and career readiness.
