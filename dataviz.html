Data Viz

The 2nd Annual DataViz PREP-KC Competition invites teams of high school teachers and students from PREP-KC's partner urban districts to work together to create compelling data visualizations. This year we will have two divisions, with the open division following last years theme, FOLLOW YOUR PASSION, and the Greater Kansas City Region community impact theme where teams will be asked to create a visualization that will help PREP-KC better understand the impact of data in their community.

See last years winners and join us anytime between now and the deadline on March 31st. Start your visualization journey today! Registration is not necessary, but will allow teams to get updates, helpful resources and more.
