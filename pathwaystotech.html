Program History & Overview

PREP-KC, in partnership with Oracle (aka Cerner), designed our Pathways to Technology program to give Kansas City's urban high school students a head start on postsecondary degrees and careers in technologyrelated fields. Pathways to Technology is one of the most diverse tech-focused pipeline initiatives in the Kansas City region. 95% of Pathways to Technology students and alumni identify as Black, Latinx, or people of color, and 38% of our students and alumni are female. Pathways to Technology combines expert computer science instruction & micro-credentials with realworld projects on Oracle Cerner's Innovations campus. This unique approach to computer science (CS) instruction prepares our students to excel in tech-focused internships and careers.

Results & Highlights

  • 96% of students earned industry-recognized credentials
  • 80% completed at least one client-connected project
  • 50% participated in internship or work-based learning
  • Students showed a 25% increase in STEM interest
  • Over 300 students impacted across the metro

What's Next?

  • Integrating opportunities for students to master data science skills.
  • Increasing the number of client-connected projects and community impact projects that students complete.
  • Launching additional internship and micro-credential opportunities in cybersecurity, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data visualization.
