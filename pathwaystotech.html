Program History & Overview

PREP-KC, in partnership with Oracle (aka Cerner), designed our Pathways to Technology program to give Kansas City's urban high school students a head start on postsecondary degrees and careers in technology related fields.

Pathways to Technology is one of the most diverse tech-focused pipeline initiatives in the Kansas City region. 95% of Pathways to Technology students and alumni identify as Black, Latinx, or people of color, and 38% of our students and alumni are female.

Pathways to Technology combines expert computer science instruction & micro-credentials with realworld projects on Oracle Cerner's Innovations campus. This unique approach to computer science (CS) instruction prepares our students to excel in tech-focused internships and careers.

What's Next?

Prep-KC aims to scale the program to serve more high schools and students across the region.

New modules will be added to help students explore real-world data sets and develop data analysis skills.

Students will engage with more industry partners to solve meaningful, real-world problems that impact local communities.

Students will access credentialed training in high-demand tech areas, enchancing their college and career readiness.
