Evergy Client Project

As part of a collaborative project with Evergy, our team is focusing on strategies to engage the Kansas City community and attract interest in career opportunities as linemen. Our goal is to highlight the essential role of linemen at Evergy and the rewarding career path they offer.

Our efforts have materialized into a draft PDF showcasing a potential website dedicated to this initiative. The website aims to be a comprehensive resource, providing detailed information on the lineman profession, the impact they have on the community, and the benefits of pursuing a career with Evergy. Access the draft website design here.

To bolster our approach with quantitative data, we have devised a survey that will be distributed to both students and adults in the Kansas City area. The insights gathered from this survey will inform a data-driven analysis, enabling us to tailor our strategies effectively. We're looking to understand public perception, career aspirations, and the motivational factors that could encourage individuals to consider a career as an Evergy lineman. The draft of this survey can be reviewed here.

We anticipate that the findings from our analysis will provide Evergy with actionable recommendations to reach potential candidates and foster a greater understanding and appreciation of this vital profession within the community.
